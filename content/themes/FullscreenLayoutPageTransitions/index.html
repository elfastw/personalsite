Google Preferred HTML Flipbook

Youtube Preferred is a advertising package offered to advertisers by Youtube that bundles its most successful channels into targeted demographic packages. Google was looking for a way to inform its sales staff and potential advertisers about this platform and its offerings. The idea was to create an interactive flipbook based on HTML5 Canvas and Javascript technologies that could be presented and browsed through as a informative and interesting way to keep staff up to date on current packages.

Clinique HTML5 Banners

Managed development of all HTML5 and Rich Media Banners used by Clinique. A large and complex project with many deliverables and specifications. Banners were created using html canvas and createjs. Complex creatives and heavy files necessitated coming up with creative solutions to file size issues. Developed a method to deliver complex banners as one html file with base64 image encoding and inline javascript.

Blundstone Internal Sales iPad App

Blundstone prides itself on the comfort and durability of its boots, as it expanded in to the US market it needed a way for it sales reps to place orders at retailers while offering the opportunity to discover the brand story and browse the entire product line. Owned and managed development of an application that lets the sales staff share that history while taking orders in store.

Bulova Website Network

Sites that were developed included bulova.com, bulovaaccuswiss.com, caravelleny.com, bulova-manutd.com, bulovacorporatesales.com, bulovaprecisionist.com, and bulovaclocks.com. Massive global project in 13 languages and post launch support.
